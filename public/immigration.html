Visas

There are many visas that you may be eligible for. Below are some of the most popular visa options. For more information on these and other visas for which you may be eligible, schedule a consultation.

EB-5

EB-5 visas are a form of investor visas are granted to foreign investors who are investing in American businesses which will generate employment as a result.

L-1

L-1 visas are granted to persons who have been employed by a foreign company in a managerial or executive position, or one involving specialized knowledge.

E-1/E-2

E-1 visas are granted to individuals pursuing trade between the United States and a foreign country with which the United States has a trade treaty.

E-2 visas are granted to individulas of trade treaty countries who make a substantial investment in a business in the United States.

O-1

O-1 visas are granted to foreign nationals of extraordinary ability in the sciences, art, education, business, or athletics, certain foreign nationals accompanying or assisting them, and their family members.

H1-B

H-1B visas are granted to persons coming to the United States to work in a specialty occupation, requiring at least a bachelor’s degree or its equivalent.

F-1

F-1 visas allow foreign students to come to the United States for purposes of pursuing studies at any level, from Elementary School through University and post-graduate degrees.
